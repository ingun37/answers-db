## Inverse

If ${\langle a,b \rangle} \in \cup_{{\langle a,b \rangle} \in D_1 \cap D_2} D_{\langle a,b \rangle}$  
then it's like ${\langle a,b \rangle} \in D_{\langle a,b \rangle}$.  
We proved $D_{\langle a,b \rangle} \subset D_1 \cap D_2$ in (i)
