Let $`(X,\tau)`$ be the subspace of $`\mathbb{R}`$ given by $`X = [0,1] \cup [2,4]`$.

Define $`f:(X,\tau) \rightarrow \mathbb{R}`$ by

```math
f(x) = \begin{dcases}
   1, &\text{if } x \in [0,1] \\
   2, &\text{if } x \in [2,4]
\end{dcases}
```

Prove that $`f`$ is continuous. (Does this surprise you?)
