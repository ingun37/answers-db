Because $`X`$ and $`Y`$ are independent

```math
\begin{aligned}
  &P(X\leq 2 \textrm{  and   } Y\leq 2) \\
  &=P(X\leq 2)P(Y\leq 2) \\
  &=(P_X(1) + P_X(2))(P_Y(1) + P_Y(2)) \\
  &=({1 \over 4} + {1 \over 8})({1 \over 6} + {1 \over 6}) \\
  &=({3 \over 8})({1 \over 3}) \\
  &={17 \over 24} \\
\end{aligned}
```
