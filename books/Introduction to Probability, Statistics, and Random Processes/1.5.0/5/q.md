Let $`A = \{1,2, \cdots , 100\}`$. For any $`i \in \mathbb{N}`$, Define $`A_i`$ as the set of numbers in $`A`$ that are divisible by $`i`$. For example:
```math
A_2 = \{ 2, 4, 6, \cdots, 100 \} \\
A_3 = \{ 3, 6, 9, \cdots, 99 \} \\
```

a. Find $`|A_2|, |A_3|, |A_4|, |A_5|`$  
b. Find $`|A_2 \cup A_3 \cup A_5|`$
