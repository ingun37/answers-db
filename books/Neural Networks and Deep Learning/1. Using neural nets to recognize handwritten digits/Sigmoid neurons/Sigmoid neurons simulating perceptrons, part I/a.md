```math
\begin{aligned}
&kw\cdot x + kb \leq 0 \\

&\rightarrow k^{-1}k(w\cdot x + b) \leq k^{-1}0 \\
&\rightarrow w\cdot x + b \leq 0 \\
\end{aligned}
```
